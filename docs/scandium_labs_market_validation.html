Validation verdict — Strong
The market is real and accelerating — CuspAI's $100M Series A, IBM and Microsoft entering, and Insilico's proven timeline compression all confirm institutional belief in AI materials discovery. Scandium Labs sits in the right space at the right time. The beachhead strategy is clear: win one niche first (solid-state electrolytes, perovskite stability, or PFAS alternatives), build published results through government grants, then expand. The $5.7T upstream chemicals market means even a 0.01% capture of R&D software spend is a $570M revenue opportunity.
